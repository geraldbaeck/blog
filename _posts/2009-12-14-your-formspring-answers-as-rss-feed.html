Formspring seems to be next hot social media shit, but it lacks of certain important features especially a RSS-Feed. So I decided to build one for myself and everyone who wants to use it. For example you can find my Formspring Page at:

http://www.formspring.me/geraldbaeck

And the RSS-Feed is at:

http://formspring.socialmediainferno.com/rss/rss.py?user=geraldbaeck

You can make an educated guess to get your Formspring RSS-Feed!-)
